First famous chef: Pierre Hermé

When you’re dubbed as “The Picasso of Pastry,” it’s clear you have an incredible talent. Pierre Hermé is the ‘Picasso’ of pastries. He is notably most famous for his flavoured macarons that are rich and unusual in taste.His baking skills have made him well known and he has written over a dozen books. He is the youngest person to ever be named “France’s Pastry Chef of the Year”. Currently, he manages two Michelin star awarded restaurants in France.
